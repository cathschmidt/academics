Philosophy

The philosophy of the program and the areas of focus that the undergraduates will go through.

Studio Culture

At the beginning of each year students reserve a home space that will be the place in which they do their work. These home spaces are not only integral to giving students access to equipment, but to one another. A culture of discourse and critique amongst students as well as a strong social bond is part of what makes Architecture at RISD unique.

These spaces are shared between undergraduate and graduate students which encourages a great sharing of ideas.
